Accessibility in the park
"Your present circumstances don't determine where you can go; they merely determine where you start." - Nido Qubein

At Ocean Prime, we provide visitors with disability a rich experience of exploring the vast ocean kingdom at their own pace and assistance, if desired. In order to get assistance please inform the staff prior to your arrival at the park, by contacting us through an email or phone no., so that we could make arrangements to ensure you don't miss anything.

Rides and Festival Specials

Rides for Everyone

With our high-speed Roller Coasters experience the extraordinary feeling of weightlessness as you dive deeper from 100 feet altitude. Capture memories of spectacular view from the Ferris Wheel as it moves up, taking you to the paradise. "Silent of the Sea" could be mysterious but at the same time full of Peace and Beauty, experiance this feeling of Tranquility with our Boat Ride.

Opening Hours: 8:00 am to 3:00 pm

Type of rides: Roller coasters, Water Slides, Jet Ski, Surfing, Ferris Wheel, Boat Ride

Festival Specials

Christmas and New Year's Eve are some of most beautiful moments we experiance every year. Make these special days into unforgettable moments of Happiness by being part of Carnival of Happiness, spectacular Fireworks and mesmerizing view of park at Chrismas night and New Year's Eve along with live Concert featuring special musical artists.

Opening Hours: 11:00 am to 5:00 pm

Types: Merry Christmas, Fireworks, New Year's Eve, Carnival, Concert

Meet Our Ocean Friends

How much are our ocean freinds talented? Curious! Discover some of most surprising things that they can do. Watching dolphins through the screen is not bad but what if you could go beyond the screen and watch them not just swim in water but also doing some dance or maybe some stunts. Discover the hidden talents of not just dolphins but of several sea animals(which are generally known for their leisure not agility) as you explore assets we have in store for you.

Opening Hours: 3:00 pm to 5:00 pm

Types: Dancing Penguins, Appointment with Sharks, Dolphins Adventure, Encounter with Killer Whales, Sea Lion Kingdom

Crystal Ballet

Enjoy not just a dance but experience some of your favourite characters from our Queen Elsa to beautiful Giselle, by drowning in the mesmerising performance of talented ballerinas as they sway on the stage, reincarnating the wonders from Swan Lake to Frozen.

Opening Hours: 7:00 pm to 11:00 pm

Step into the Realm of Ocean

Aquater

Experience the feeling of being part of the Ocean Kingdom. Enjoy delicious marine-theme based food with your family in company of your favorite sea creatures from adorable Beluga Whale and Dolphins to thrilling Killer Whales.

Featured Guests: Beluga Whale, Great White Sharks, Dolphins, Killer Whales, Green Sea Turtle, Big-Eye Sharks, Star Fish, Octopus, Jelly fish

Ocean Galaxy

Where is the deepest point of Earth? Ocean? Yes, Mariana Trench (a part of ocean) is deepest point of Earth. Who lives there? Nobody? Although, it may look strange, there are some of the most unusual animals found in Mariana Trench like Dumbo Octopus, Angeler Fish, Comb Jelly,etc. At Ocean Galaxy, experience some of the most unusual inhabitants of vast Ocean Kingdom.

Opening Hours: 8:00 am to 11:00 am

Type of species: Tunas, Jelly Fish, Sharks, Killer Whale, Sea Horse, Starfishes, Perl Oyster, Octopus, Dumbo Octopus, Comb Jellies , Deep Sea Hatchet Fish, Angelerfish, Pirahna, Blue whale and so much more.
